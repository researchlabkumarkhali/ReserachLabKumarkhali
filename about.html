Our Story

Research Lab Kumarkhali was founded in 2019 with a simple mission: to make science and technology accessible to every student. What started as a small workshop in Kumarkhali has now grown into a full-fledged project laboratory serving students across Bangladesh.

We noticed that many students struggle with their science fair projects, semester assignments, and final year projects due to lack of proper guidance and resources. Our team of engineers and educators came together to bridge this gap.

Today, we've helped over 100 students successfully complete their projects, win science fairs, and achieve academic excellence. Our commitment to quality and innovation remains unchanged.

Our Mission

To empower students with practical knowledge and hands-on experience in science and technology through innovative projects. We strive to make learning fun, accessible, and impactful.

Our Vision

To become the leading science project laboratory in Bangladesh, inspiring the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators. We envision a future where every student has access to quality STEM education.
